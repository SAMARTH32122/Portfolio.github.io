Project

Role: Team member

Title: INVESTIGATIONS ON FRICTION STIR WELDING OF NYLON MICRO-PARTICLE ENHANCED 3D PRINTED PARTS APPLIED TO THE WINGS OF UNMANNED AERIAL VEHICLE

Description: Due to the size restriction of the 3D printing machine, a component with large size and volume is difficult to be produced on it, so in this project, smaller 3D printed parts are joined together i.e., large-sized components are produced in sections and then they are joined/welded together. Further, the welding section is enhanced with nylon microparticles to investigate its effect on Friction stir welding.To confirm the improvements, mechanical tests including tensile and hardness are performed on the weld section. Later, the optimized results obtained by Design of Experiments (DOE) are applied to weld the wings of a UAV, thereby attaining a large-sized wing.
